Angela Chantre Astaiza is an associate professor at the University of Cauca in Popayán, Colombia. She is member of the Faculty of Accounting, Economics and Administrative Sciences where she is head of the Department of Tourism Sciences. She obtained her PhD in Business Economics and Finance (2017) and Master in Marketing (2009) from the Universidad Rey Juan Carlos in Madrid, Spain. She is a Business Administrator (2004) from the University of Cauca in Colombia. She is currently classified by Colciencias (now the Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation) as a Researcher in the Associate category. She has worked as Academic Evaluator for the Ministry of National Education in Colombia. She has been an evaluator of project calls for several universities in the areas of marketing, business administration, business management, innovation, tourism, organizations, cultural management, technology applied to various sectors and teaching innovation. She has worked on projects related to tourism technology, tourism data analysis, cultural heritage management, moocs, international cooperation, data analysis. She has been a reviewer for multiple national and international journals.

His main interests include Marketing, mainly, tourism marketing, Tourism, administration, Cultural Management, applied technology, data analysis, educational quality, among others.
